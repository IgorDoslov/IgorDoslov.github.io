About:

Demon King is a 3rd Person online multiplayer game that was created in a team of 7 known as Dead Bear Games. The game involves having the ability to gain power by devouring minions or players to evolve into a greater being. Players must gain the most amount of points by the end of the game to win. We are releasing the game in Feburary 2022 on steam.
